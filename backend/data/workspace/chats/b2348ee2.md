## Key Facts
- The water cycle describes how water moves around the planet, changing form and location.
- Evaporation is when water turns into an invisible gas (water vapor) due to heat.
- Condensation is when water vapor cools and changes back into tiny water droplets or ice crystals, forming clouds.
- Precipitation is when water falls back to Earth from the atmosphere, with rain being a common example.
